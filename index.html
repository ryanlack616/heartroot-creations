Shattered Reflection

Mixed Media on Canvas

A woman screams into a broken mirror. Her face fractures across the shards — distorted, stretched, impossible to look away from. Deep red bleeds through the cracks like a wound finally exposed. At the bottom, barely visible, a child curls up beneath the shattering.

They are the same person.

The gold that fills the fracture lines isn't healing — it's the years. Everything built on top of the break to keep going. Beautiful from the outside. But still filling cracks.

"I broke but I didn't scatter."

About
